Examination Security

Standard Operating Procedure 2025

Strategic Rationale

The Mission

Every invigilator is an assessment guardian. Our goal is an environment defined by:

Absolute Security

Secure chain of custody.

Uncompromising Fairness

Identical conditions for all.

Rigid Standardization

Exact adherence to 2025 frameworks.

Staffing Ratios

  • 1 : 20 Invigilator to Student Ratio
  • Minimum 2 Invigilators present at ALL times
  • No Subject Teachers Cannot invigilate own subject.

Risk Mitigation: Roles

Lead Invigilator

Operational commander. Verbal instructions, timing, and packet integrity.

Circulating Invigilator

Active patrolling. Eliminates blind spots. Escorts for bathroom breaks.

Monitoring Invigilator

Seated at rear. Creates psychological deterrent via observation.

Floating Invigilator

Supports max 3 rooms. Must remain immediately contactable. Break in contact = Breach of compliance.

Physical Room Preparation

  • Desk Spacing: Exactly 1.5 meters apart.
  • Directionality: All desks face same direction. No facing tables.
  • Visibility: Large clock & Start/Finish times clearly displayed.
  • Remove/Cover all instructional displays (maps, periodic tables).

Mandatory Signage (1 Week Prior)

Notice to Candidates Items Not Permitted Silence Please

Material Compliance

Permitted

  • Blue or Black Pen
  • Pencils (2B/B) for graphs
  • Ruler, Eraser, Protractor
  • Bilingual Dictionary (non-Lang exams)
  • *Calculators only if allowed by paper

Prohibited

  • GEL PENS (Cannot Scan)
  • Correction Fluid/Tape
  • Smart Watches / Phones
  • Scratch Paper (Use booklets)
  • Un-cleared Calculators

Standardized Timeline

1

Pre-Arrival & Admittance

10-min silent admittance. Verify identity. Collect unauthorized materials.

2

The "Open"

Open sealed packets in front of students.

3

Reading Time (5 Mins)

Mandatory. NO writing, NO highlighting, NO calculators.

4

The Verbal Start

Lead Invigilator announces: "Your reading time is over... you may start to write."

5

The Conclusion

Immediate "Stop" command. Collect all scripts before dismissal.

Contingency Matrix

Late Arrival (<1 hr)
Admit. Allow full time. Report via DP/CP Late Candidate survey.
Late Arrival (>1 hr)
Admit at Coordinator discretion. Contact IB Answers immediately. No grade guaranteed.
Academic Misconduct
Confiscate discreetly. Do NOT expel unless disruptive. Report within 24h.
Evacuation
Stop timing. Record time. Total silence. Resume in alternative venue if possible.
